FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the first consultation really free? +
Yes. We offer every prospective client a free, no-obligation initial consultation. We will listen to your matter, assess its legal dimensions, and advise you on your options — with no cost and no pressure to proceed.
Where is your office located? +
We are based in Machakos Town, Machakos County. We serve clients across the county and, for certain matters, across the wider Eastern region and Nairobi. Remote and virtual consultations are available on request.
How long will my case take? +
Timelines vary significantly by matter type and complexity. At your consultation, we will give you a realistic assessment of likely duration. We do not give false timelines — honesty about your case is fundamental to how we work.
What are your fees? +
Our fees are structured clearly and communicated upfront before you commit to anything. We offer fixed-fee arrangements for certain matters and hourly billing for others. We also handle a proportion of matters on a pro bono basis for eligible clients.
Can I switch lawyers mid-case to come to you? +
Yes. You have the right to change your legal representation at any time. We handle such transitions with care and professionalism, conducting a thorough review of your case before proceeding to ensure continuity of advocacy.
Do you handle cases outside Machakos County? +
Yes. While Machakos is our home base, we regularly appear before the High Court and Court of Appeal in Nairobi, and we advise clients with interests across Kenya. Complex cross-border matters are handled on a case-by-case basis.
Is my information kept confidential? +
Absolutely. Attorney-client privilege is sacred to our practice. Everything you share with us is held in strict confidence, governed by our professional obligations and Kenya's data privacy legislation.
Do you offer payment plans? +
We understand that legal matters can arise unexpectedly. In appropriate circumstances, we are willing to discuss structured payment arrangements to ensure that quality legal representation remains accessible to our community.
